Research Interests

My research focuses on developing advanced thermal management solutions for heterogeneously integrated electronics, addressing both macro‑ and micro‑scale challenges. At the macro level, I design vertical power delivery architectures with substrate‑embedded microfluidic cooling to support thermally sustainable operation in 3D‑stacked processors. At the micro level, I investigate high‑thermal‑conductivity thin films as heat spreaders for localized hotspots, employing rapid, low‑temperature, low‑pressure deposition techniques. I am also developing a machine learning–accelerated multi‑scale modeling framework that speeds up spatio‑temporal temperature predictions in FinFET arrays for real‑time layout optimization.
