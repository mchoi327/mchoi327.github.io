Research Interests

My research focuses on developing advanced thermal management solutions for heterogeneously integrated electronics, addressing both macro‑ and micro‑scale challenges. At the macro level, I design vertical power delivery architectures with substrate‑embedded microfluidic cooling. At the micro level, I investigate high‑thermal‑conductivity thin films as heat spreaders and develop a machine‑learning–accelerated multi‑scale modeling framework for FinFET arrays.
