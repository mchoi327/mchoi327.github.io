Mingeun receives the “Most First Author Journal Papers” Topping Fellowship

September 18, 2025

The George W. Woodruff School of Mechanical Engineering awarded Mingeun Choi the “Most First Author Journal Papers” Topping Fellowship. The fellowship recognizes students who have published the greatest number of first-author journal papers based on research performed at Georgia Tech. The total award amount is $3,500.

The paper “Automated Electro-Thermal Modeling Framework of Distributed Vertical Power Delivery Architectures with Substrate-Embedded Microfluidic Cooling” presents an automated electro-thermal modeling framework that demonstrates the benefits of distributed vertical power delivery and substrate-embedded microfluidic cooling.
